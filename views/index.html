Why I ♥ U Matters

When you fall in love, you think everything will be easy – love conquers all. The endorphins are flowing and everything is amazing

But, in time you realize no mater how strong the love is, real love takes a LOT of work and as time goes on there are a lot of other things demanding your attention – kids, work, daily chores…it is easy to forget the small things like sending an, “I love you more” note.

That’s where this site comes in. Take a moment and send a not to your special someone to remind them that no matter what is happening, the original love you both felt is still there and stronger than ever…it has just evolved into something even better.

I love You More lets you send a message to your love and it will remain forever to be looked at and shared through the good times and not so good times. Some sample messages are below, but you can create your own and continue the love story you created.
